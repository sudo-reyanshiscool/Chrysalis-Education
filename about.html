I. About

A quiet place for serious study.

Named for the chrysalis — that patient, hidden stage of transformation — our centre was founded in 2007 on a simple conviction: that command of the English language is cultivated slowly, in conversation with experienced teachers and enduring texts.

Our tutors are career educators, drawn from universities and secondary schools, who have spent decades reading closely and teaching with care. Every lesson is prepared. Every student is read.

We do not chase trends. We do not shortcut the work. What we offer is older and plainer than that: attention, time, and the patient company of people who love the language.

Our Tutors

Experienced teachers, quietly devoted.

Every member of our faculty has taught English for more than a decade — in classrooms, lecture halls, or one-to-one tutorials. They read widely, mark thoroughly, and speak plainly. They are the heart of Chrysalis.

Our Team

The tutors of Chrysalis.

A small faculty of readers, writers, and patient teachers — each devoted to a particular corner of the English tradition.

Kavita

Founder & Head of Wholistic Development

Kavita draws on years of teaching at The British School New Delhi to shape an approach that reaches beyond academics into how students think, communicate, and carry themselves. That philosophy is the foundation everything here is built on.

Nikasha

Lead IGCSE & IB Educator

Nikasha is a proud alumna of The British School New Delhi, giving her a rare insider’s view of the very curricula she now teaches. She pairs that firsthand experience with a warm but demanding classroom style that pushes students toward genuine mastery rather than exam shortcuts.

Supriya

Primary Educator

Supriya turns early learning into something children actually look forward to. Her classroom is structured but joyful, laying the groundwork in literacy, numeracy, and confidence that every older student eventually looks back and thanks her for.
