I. About

A quiet place for serious study.

Named for the chrysalis — that patient, hidden stage of transformation — our centre was founded in 2007 on a simple conviction: that command of the English language is cultivated slowly, in conversation with experienced teachers and enduring texts.

Our tutors are career educators, drawn from universities and secondary schools, who have spent decades reading closely and teaching with care. Every lesson is prepared. Every student is read.

II. Services

Programs of study.

01

Literature Seminars

Close reading of poetry, drama, and the novel — from Chaucer to the contemporary — in small, discussion-led groups.

02

Composition & Style

Weekly essay instruction grounded in classical rhetoric: argument, clarity, cadence, and the careful revision of one's own prose.

03

Examination Preparation

Focused tutelage for IGCSE, IB, A-Level, and university entrance examinations, with attention to both technique and substance.

04

Private Tutorials

One-to-one sessions tailored to the student's reading life — whether nurturing a gifted writer or rebuilding fundamentals with patience.
