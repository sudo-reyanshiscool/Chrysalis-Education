II. Services

Programs of study.

Enquiries

Frequently asked.

What age groups do you teach?

We work with students from roughly age ten through university entrance — from early literature appreciation to advanced composition and exam preparation.

Are sessions one-to-one or in groups?

Both. Literature seminars are small discussion-led groups (typically four to six students); composition and examination preparation are offered either privately or in pairs.

Do you offer online tuition?

Yes. Students outside Delhi attend by video, with shared documents for live annotation. In-person sessions remain available at the studio.

How are fees structured?

Fees vary by programme, format, and frequency. Please write or call for a detailed proposal — we are happy to tailor a plan to the student.

How do I begin?

Arrange a brief consultation through the contact page, WhatsApp, or a phone call. We will discuss the student's goals and recommend a course of study.

Which examination boards do you prepare students for?

A Level, O Level, IGCSE, IB, CBSE, and ICSE — across Cambridge, Edexcel, and Indian boards.
